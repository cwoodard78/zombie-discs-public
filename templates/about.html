About Zombie Discs

Welcome to Zombie Discs – the community-driven platform for disc golfers to recover and reunite with lost discs! Whether you're an avid disc golfer or just starting out, we aim to make your game more enjoyable by ensuring your favorite discs don't stay lost forever.

Our Mission

At Zombie Discs, we believe every disc deserves a second chance. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform where players can log lost or found discs, helping fellow golfers recover their gear quickly and hassle-free.

How It Works

  • Log a Disc: Whether you’ve lost or found a disc, simply log it into our database with relevant details.
  • Find Matches: Use our platform to search for logged discs and reconnect them with their rightful owners. You will even be automatically notified when a new match is found!
  • Community Focus: Collaborate with other disc golfers and share good karma to make the sport even more enjoyable!

Why Zombie Discs?

Losing a beloved disc is frustrating, but we’re here to make the recovery process easier. By using GPS tagging and a supportive community, Zombie Discs makes sure your gear keeps coming back – just like a zombie!

Get Involved

Ready to join the Zombie Discs community? Sign up today and start logging your discs. Together, we can make disc golf better for everyone!
